## 👋 About Me

I recently received my master's degree in applied mathematics from National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, where I worked on combinatorics and matroid theory under the supervision of Prof. Chi Ho Yuen.

I will soon join the Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica, as an R&D Substitute Service researcher. Alongside my continuing interest in matroid theory, I am interested in exploring programming languages, type theory, and semantics.

> ✨ I am interested in how local combinatorial data can force global algebraic or geometric structure.

## 🔍 Research Interests

* **Matroid Theory:** White's conjecture, paving and split matroids, relaxation operations, and matroid toric ideals
* **Realization Spaces:** phased matroids, rigidity phenomena, and Grassmannian strata
* **Topics I Hope to Explore:** homotopy type theory, programming language semantics, and algebraic geometry

## 🧶 Featured Work

### White's Conjecture for Paving Matroids

**Yu-Chuan Yu** and Chi Ho Yuen
*Preprint, 2025*

We prove the combinatorial version of White's conjecture for paving matroids using stressed hyperplane relaxations.

[📙 arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.04163) ·
[📄 PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.04163) ·
[📝 BibTeX](https://arxiv.org/bibtex/2510.04163)

### Rigid Realization Spaces of Non-Chirotopal Phased Matroids

**Yu-Chuan Yu**
*Manuscript*

We give a sharp combinatorial criterion for projective rigidity of realizable non-chirotopal phased matroids.
